# High Availability & Resilience

## 🔥 Golden Rule

> Always design across multiple AZs

---

## 🧠 Techniques

### 1. Multi-AZ
- Automatic failover

### 2. Load Balancer
- Distributes traffic

### 3. Auto Scaling
- Handles spikes

---

## 🧪 Example

Highly available web app:

- ALB
- EC2 (Multi-AZ)
- RDS Multi-AZ
